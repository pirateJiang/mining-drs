Layer 1: The Mathematical Primitives (drs.variables)These are the data structures that hold the state of your universe.1. drs.Level (The Integrator)Purpose: Tracks continuous accumulation or depletion over time (e.g., Stockpile mass, Cyanide consumed).How it works: It has a value (current amount), a rate (speed of change), and critical thresholds (upper and lower limits).The Math: It tells the engine exactly when it will hit a boundary: $\Delta t = \frac{\text{Threshold} - \text{Value}}{\text{Rate}}$.2. drs.Timer (The Clock)Purpose: A specialized subclass of Level used to track campaign lengths, equipment warmup times, or maintenance delays.How it works: It is simply a drs.Level where the rate is permanently locked to 1.0.3. drs.State (The Instantaneous Variable)Purpose: Holds values that do not accumulate over time, but change instantly when an event occurs (e.g., "Current operating mode", "Current block grade", "Belief routing fraction").How it works: It has a value, but no rate and no thresholds. The engine ignores State objects when calculating time jumps.Layer 2: The Physical Structure (drs.module)Variables cannot float in a vacuum. They must be grouped into logical physical units.4. drs.Module (The Base Container)Purpose: The abstract building block of your digital twin. Represents a physical asset (MineFace, Crusher, Stockpile) or a cyber asset (SensorNetwork).Responsibilities:Hold collections of Levels and States.update_rates(): Recalculate how fast fluids are moving based on the current configuration.check_transitions(): Listen for the engine to announce a threshold has been hit, and react to it (e.g., "The timer hit 24 hours, so reset the daily throughput state").Layer 3: The Flow Architecture (drs.network) (New)This layer removes hardcoded physics from your modules and makes the framework universal.5. drs.Edge (The Pipe)Purpose: Moves a MaterialFlow vector (Mass rate, Metal rate, Cyanide rate) from one Module to another.6. drs.Node (The Junction)Purpose: A specialized Module that enforces the Law of Conservation. It automatically sums up incoming Edge rates, subtracts outgoing Edge rates, and applies the net difference to its internal drs.Level stockpiles.7. drs.Network (The Router)Purpose: Looks at all Nodes and Edges, performs a topological sort, and guarantees that upstream physics (the mine) calculate their rates before downstream physics (the plant) try to use them.Layer 4: Logic & Control (drs.modes & controllers)This is the "Brain" layer that alters the physics based on rules.8. OperatingModePurpose: A rigid, predefined configuration of the physical plant (e.g., ModeA, ModeA_Contingency).How it works: When applied, an OperatingMode sets the target_rates across the network. It dictates what the downstream plant demands, forcing the upstream mine to match it.9. BaseBlendingController (The Heuristic Brain)Purpose: An autonomous agent that monitors the SensorNetwork's belief states. If a belief state crosses a critical threshold, it swaps the OperatingMode.Layer 5: The Time Lord (drs.engine)This is the core execution loop that makes the entire library run.10. DRSEnginePurpose: To manage time. It knows absolutely nothing about mining, gold, or trucks. It only knows math.The Execution Loop:Poll Rates: Ask all modules to update_rates().Calculate Minimum $\Delta t$: Look at every drs.Level in the simulation. Ask each one: "Based on your current rate, how many seconds until you hit your upper or lower threshold?" 3.  Step Time: Find the smallest $\Delta t$ in the entire system. Advance the global simulation clock by exactly that amount.Update Values: Multiply every rate by $\Delta t$ and add it to the current values (e.g., $V_{\text{new}} = V_{\text{old}} + (\text{rate} \times \Delta t)$).Trigger Events: Broadcast to all modules which threshold was just hit, allowing them to change states or modes. Repeat.Layer 6: The AI Interface (environments/)This is the outermost wrapper that bridges the continuous-time physics engine to standard discrete-time Reinforcement Learning.11. RequireDecision (The Exception)Purpose: A custom Python exception thrown by the Controller when it encounters an ambiguous operational state (like a campaign shutdown) and doesn't know what to do next.How it works: It immediately halts the DRSEngine loop without crashing the physics.12. MiningRLEnv (The Gym Wrapper)Purpose: Translates the DRS world for an RL Agent (like PPO or SAC).How it works: 1. The agent calls step(action).2. The wrapper passes the action to the controller.3. The wrapper tells the DRSEngine to run.4. The engine runs forward in continuous time, perfectly resolving all thresholds and automated contingencies, until it hits the next RequireDecision flag.5. The wrapper calculates the reward based on the elapsed $\Delta t$ and returns the new observation state to the agent.
